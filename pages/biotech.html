Biotech

FlexSea is dedicated to creating sustainable processes that utilise the full biomass of the seaweed.

This passion for circularity has given rise to our Biotechnology vertical which sees us use natural practices such as bacterial fermentation to extract the full wealth of available material from our seaweed sources. Through utilising waste material from our bioplastics vertical and by-products from other seaweed industries (such as agar and carrageenan extraction) we are going beyond this to regenerate materials that can be cycled back into these industries, reducing the demand for virgin materials.

Our three biopolymers are from three distinct categories of biomolecules and encompass a diverse range of applications from cosmetics to packaging, filters to waste water treatments

For more information on our biotech products and how they can be used by your business please get in touch via our Enquiries page.
